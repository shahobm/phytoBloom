Spring Blooms in Shallow Water


Adjust the sliders to the left to change the irradiance, respiration levels. Once sufficient values are reached for the mathematical model, the tank will change color to describe a spring bloom.


Experiment 3 examines a scenario with a significantly shallow water level where the seafloor acts as a mixing layer depth for the system and allows irradiance and respiration levels to be changed. Although this mixing layer depth cannot be changed, the irradiance and respiration levels can be selected to show a bloom.
