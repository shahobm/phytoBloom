Spring Bloom Timing



Adjust the sliders to the left to change the respiration, and mixing layer depth. Once sufficient values are reached for the mathematical model, the tank will change color to describe a spring bloom. The subsequent mixing layer depth in relation to the time of year along the x-axis describes when such a bloom may occur for these conditions.


This experiment allows varying of the respiration and mixing layer depth where irradiance is fixed but shows how January to July and how a critical depth level may vary through this time period.
